title	BIRNLex UBO

foundry	No
status	Production and review
download	
source	http://birnlex.nbirn.net/ontology/BIRNLex-OBO-UBO.owl
home	http://openccdb.org/wiki/index.php/Disease_Ontology
documentation	
contact	Sarah Maynard	smaynard	ncmir.ucsd.edu
format	owl
description	This is a proposed upper biomedial ontology derived by examples from the Biodynamic Ontology, the Simple Upper Bio Ontology, the Ontology of Biomedical Reality, and the Foundational Model of Anatomy.  It is designed to be a very slim layer intervening between the Basic Formal Ontology (BFO) and the generic, domain, and application ontologies developed to formally represent entities relevant to biomedical research and clinical practice.  The intension is for their to be a shared layer for all of these ontologies to use to declares a biological context from which they can all derive required classes thereby making the equivalent foundational entities 100% algorithmically commensurate across all of these ontological artifacts.  At this date (2006-10-06), this is just a proposal meant for: review by the community of OBO Foundry-associated biomedical ontology developers; very sparse and preliminary with the bulk of the coverage in the domain of biomaterial and immaterial objects; immediate use by the BIRN Ontology Task Force with the expectation this normalized layer will expedite future integration/import of the upper level biomedical ontology eventually adopted and endorsed by the OBO Foundry participant ontology development community members.
